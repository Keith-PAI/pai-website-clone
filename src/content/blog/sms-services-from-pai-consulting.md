At PAI Consulting, we bring decades of aviation experience to Safety Management System implementation and support. Whether you're starting from scratch or enhancing an existing program, our team can help.

## Our SMS Services

**Gap Analysis** — We assess your current safety processes against 14 CFR Part 5 and ICAO standards to identify what's working and what needs development.

**Implementation Support** — From safety policy development to hazard identification processes, we help you build each SMS component methodically.

**Training** — Our upcoming PAI Academy will offer comprehensive SMS training through self-paced and hybrid formats. In the meantime, we provide custom training tailored to your organization.

**Documentation** — We help develop the manuals, procedures, and forms your SMS needs to function effectively.

**Ongoing Consulting** — SMS isn't a one-time project. We provide ongoing support for safety assurance activities, risk assessments, and program refinement.

## Why PAI?

Our team includes senior aviation analysts, former pilots, and regulatory specialists who understand SMS not just as a compliance requirement, but as a practical framework for managing safety. We bring real operational experience to every engagement.

Every organization's SMS journey is different. Contact us to discuss where you are today and where you want to be. We'll help you chart the most efficient path forward.
